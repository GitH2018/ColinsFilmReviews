ABOUT ME ....

Hi ... my name is Colin ...

I've all been in love with film from before I can remember,
films of all sorts and genres ... all shapes and sizes ...

During my viewing experiences obviously I've liked
some more than others, and there are some I am glad
to see the back of ... and others I would happily
watch over and over again!

I thought it would be a good idea to create some film
reviews (both good and bad!) for some of the films
I've seen - hoping these reviews may encourage some
to watch and enjoy more films and also hinting at
some others that I think are best avoided!!!

I do these reviews in good faith ... and they are my
opinion only - but I hope that you find them interesting
and informative!

If these reviews have helped you please say so in the
feedback form ... and perhaps suggest or submit reviews
on your own!

Thanks for visiting the site, and for your company ...
now ... on with the reviews!!!!
